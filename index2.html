🎯 Part 1: Career Opportunities

⚡ Electronics Technician
Maintains, troubleshoots, and repairs electronic equipment and systems in industrial settings. Works with circuit boards, testing equipment, and diagnostic tools.
Circuit Analysis Troubleshooting Testing Equipment Safety Standards
🤖 Industrial Automation Specialist
Programs PLCs, designs control systems, and maintains automated industrial processes. Specializes in SCADA systems and industrial communication protocols.
PLC Programming SCADA HMI Design Industrial Networks
🔧 Circuit Design Engineer
Designs electronic circuits, PCB layouts, and develops electronic prototypes. Works with CAD software and simulation tools for circuit optimization.
PCB Design CAD Software Circuit Simulation Component Selection
✅ Quality Control Inspector
Tests electronic components for safety standards and quality assurance. Ensures compliance with international standards and regulations.
Quality Standards Testing Protocols Documentation Compliance
🔩 Maintenance Engineer
Diagnoses and repairs complex industrial electronic systems and equipment. Plans preventive maintenance and manages spare parts inventory.
Preventive Maintenance System Diagnostics Equipment Management Technical Documentation
